Kitri
J - Acoustic
A piano vocal unit consisting of the older sister Mona and the younger sister Hina. With a classical base, they create experimental music with a unique presence and made their major debut in January 2019 with their 1st EP "Primo" from Nippon Columbia BETTER DAYS label. They are also active in many other fields, including providing songs for other artists.
